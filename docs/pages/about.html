A bit more about who we are...

At Edwards Bros Roofing Ltd we are specialists in flat and pitched roofing.

We are approved fitters for Firestone EPDM and Alwitra Evalon flat Roofing systems. We are also licensed waste carriers which allows us to remove waste from our sites quickly and safely.

We specialise in all aspects of roofing work from small repairs to complete new roofs.

Customer satisfaction is a priority. If required, we are able to provide references from our existing happy customers.

We are fully insured and all our operatives are fully trained and qualified in their area of work.

We provide free quotations so please do not hesitate to contact us.
