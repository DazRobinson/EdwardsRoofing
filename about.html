About us...

We are a Ruthin based roofing company covering all of North Wales and North West England. We specialise in all types of roofing work ranging from historic listed buildings to complete new roofs on new dwellings.

We are approved fitters for Firestone EPDM and Alwitra Evalon flat Roofing systems.

As you can see from our gallery we carry out work for both private clients and large construction firms where our jobs can range from small alterations to multiple properties on large sites.

Customer satisfaction is a priority. If required, we are able to provide references from our existing happy customers.

We pride ourselves on providing a first class service and as a result, the majority of our business is generated through word of mouth and recommendations.

We are fully insured and all our operatives are fully trained and qualified in their area of work.

We provide free quotations so please do not hesitate to contact us.
